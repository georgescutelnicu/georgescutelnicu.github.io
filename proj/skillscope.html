Skill Scope

Streamlit-based application designed to analyze job market trends in Romania across various categories. This project scrapes jobs data from LinkedIn, processes it, and visualizes key insights, such as top technologies and tools, education levels, experience distribution etc.
Note: The data for this project was scraped on June 24, 2024. Please be aware that job market trends can change rapidly, and the insights presented in this app might become less relevant over time.
